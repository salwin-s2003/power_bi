# Day 1 – Power BI Fundamentals
## Objective
Build first Power BI dashboard using online service.

## Dataset
Manually created sales dataset (8 rows, 5 columns).

## Key Measures
- Total Sales = Quantity × UnitPrice
- Total Quantity = Sum(Quantity)

## Key Insights
- Laptops drive highest revenue.
- South region shows largest sales volume (Mouse sales).

## Skills Learned
- Upload data to Power BI Service
- Create visuals (bar, line, pie, card)
- Publish & share online
